Android Engineer, Mobile Telemetry

Maps.me

San Francisco, Washington, D.C., and Helsinki

What We Do

The Mobile Telemetry SDK team builds the open source library that powers the collection of anonymous data about the map and device location to continuously update and improve your map experience. Customers of our SDK include apps on Android, iOS, React Native, and Unity. We work with devices of all shapes and forms, from the latest flagship phone to tablets, laptops, smartwatches, HMD, and embedded boards.

What You'll Do
  • Design and implement mobile telemetry components and systems
  • Craft expressive and easy to use APIs for developers using Mobile Telemetry SDK
  • Collaborate closely with other teams across Mapbox to understand and build what is needed to enable delightful mobile developer experiences
  • Build on-device performance measurement tools for continuous optimization of our data collection mechanism
  • Own release and QA cycles of your code
What We Believe are Important Traits for This Role
  • Software engineering skills and software fundamentals
  • Knowledge of software design, systems and architecture
  • Enthusiasm for building components and systems with reliability and long term support in mind
  • Knowledge of core Java/Kotlin
  • Experience building SDKs in any language is a plus
  • Experience working with Location APIs (Android framework or Google Play Services) is a plus
  • Experience with performance measurement tools specific to Android is a plus
  • Open-minded to explore new languages, skills, techniques, and platforms
  • Desire to learn and to teach
